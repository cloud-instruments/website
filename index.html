Battery Testing
Visualization & Analytics
Empowering Battery Scientists
Battery scientists and engineers struggle with an archaic workflow characterized by high upfront capital costs, limited software capabilities, and lack of flexibility. These problems compound to form a research environment plagued by slow development time, minimal insights into data results, and susceptibility to human error.
“My engineers spend 25% of their time on data processing.”
- Dr. Michael Slater, Senior Scientist, Farasis Energy
Cloud Instruments offers software that will greatly streamline battery test data collection and analysis for battery scientists by automating many of the most time-consuming aspects of battery testing, allowing researchers to focus on interpreting their data and drawing meaningful conclusions.
Automatic Plots
Import your data files and generate plots in just a few clicks, choosing from a large selection of axes combinations.
Statistical Analysis
Quickly review your data set with statistical analysis and swap between statistical averages to create accurate graphs.
Deep Insights
Use the power of electronic computation to gain new insights from advanced analyses of your data, such as dQ/dV plots.
